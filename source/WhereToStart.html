Where To Start

All Rainier trails are very much worth hiking, but for those new to Her Majesty, here are some suggestions on where to begin.

Epic views: Burroughs Mountain, Panhandle Gap, Skyline Trail

Waterfalls: Silver Falls, Spray Falls

Wildflowers: Skyline Trail, Spray Park

Fantastic forests: Carbon River, Grove of the Patriarchs

Sunrise or sunset: Dege Peak, Fremont Lookout, Tolmie Peak

Off-season or snowshoe: Green Lake, Lakes Loop, Rampart Ridge Loop

Wheelchair accessible: Myrtle Falls, Nisqually Vista

Challenge yourself: Indian Henry's, Indian Bar, Camp Muir

Backpacking: Spray Park Loop, Northern Loop
