Where To Start
wheretostart

I am hesitant about recommending favorite hikes, for a couple of reasons:

<ol>
  <li>
    I have yet to meet a Rainier trail that didn't have something great about it.
  </li>
  <li>
    Back before I got serious about wanting to hike every trail, I found myself 
    revisiting the same favorites over and over again. This was a bad rut and I was 
    missing out on other fantastic options.
  </li>
</ol>

But everyone has to start somewhere. With the caveat that all Rainier trails 
are very much worth hiking, here are some suggestions on where to begin. Most of 
these trails are best visited from late July to September, as they'll be under 
deep snow at other times of year.
