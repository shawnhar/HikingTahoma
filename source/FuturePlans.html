Future Plans

My goal is to hike every maintained trail inside Mount Rainier National Park. Which trail I visit next can be a last minute decision depending on conditions and mood, but there are a few I particularly have my eye on at the moment:

  • Westside Road to Klapatche Park (backpacking overnighter)
  • Northern Loop (4 day backpacking loop)
  • Gobblers Knob
  • Palisades Lakes
  • Eagle Peak
  • Owyhigh Lakes
  • Shriner Peak
  • The entire Wonderland! (perhaps 2021?)

This site will be updated with increasingly more of the map colored in as I continue to explore these and other Rainier trails.
