Future Plans

My goal is to hike every maintained trail inside Mount Rainier National Park, and I'm close to achieving this. Official trails that I have yet to hike:

  • Cowlitz Divide Trail (either standalone or as part of an Eastside Loop)
  • From Gobblers Knob into the Glacier View Wilderness
  • Huckleberry Creek

Unofficial, unmaintained trails I want to attempt for fun, but that don't count toward my formal goal:

  • Goat Island Mountain
  • Knapsack Pass
  • Skyscraper Mountain

Intriguing rumors of trails that may not actually exist anymore:

  • Chenuis Falls Trail (continuing past the falls)
  • Mowich Boundary Trail
  • Nisqually Boundary Trail
  • North Backbone Ridge

If you know of any hiking trails (not climbing or scrambling routes) that I am missing, please let me know!
