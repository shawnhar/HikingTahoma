Future Plans

My goal is to hike every maintained trail inside Mount Rainier National Park. This goal is now, if not quite yet in sight, at least close enough that I can enumerate all the trails I have still to explore.

Backpacking trips:

  • The entire Wonderland! (I have a permit for September 2021)
  • Eastside Loop
  • East Eastern Loop (via PCT from Laughingwater Creek to Chinook Pass)
  • Huckleberry Creek

Day hikes:

  • Paul Peak Trail
  • Pinnacle Saddle
  • Crystal Peak
  • Silver Forest Trail
  • Naches Peak Loop
  • Gobblers Knob to Goat Lake (or perhaps I should treat this as the start of a backpacking trip in the Glacier View Wilderness?)

Things I want to do for fun, but that don't count toward my official goal:

  • Boundary Trail (not quite inside the National Park)
  • Knapsack Pass (not an officially maintained trail)
  • Skyscraper Mountain (not an officially maintained trail)
  • West Fork White River (not an officially maintained trail)

This site will be updated with increasingly more of the map colored in as I continue to work my way through this list.
