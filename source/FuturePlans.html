Future Plans

  • Cowlitz Divide Trail (either standalone or as part of an Eastside Loop)
  • From Gobblers Knob into the Glacier View Wilderness
  • Huckleberry Creek

Unofficial routes I want to attempt for fun, but that don't count toward my formal goal:

  • Bald Rock
  • Brown Peak
  • Copper Mountain
  • Cowlitz Rocks
  • Elysian Fields
  • Goat Island Mountain
  • Knapsack Pass
  • Mount Ararat
  • Pyramid Peak
  • Skyscraper Mountain
  • Sunset Park lookout site and benchmark

Intriguing rumors of once-trails that may not actually exist anymore:

  • Chenuis Falls Trail (continuing past the falls)
  • Mowich Boundary Trail

  • North Backbone Ridge

If you know of any hikeable places (not climbing or scrambling routes) that I am missing, please let me know!
