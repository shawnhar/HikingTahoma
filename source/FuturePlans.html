Future Plans

My goal is to hike every maintained trail inside Mount Rainier National Park. Exactly which trail to visit when can be a last minute decision depending on conditions and mood, but there are a few I particularly have my eye on at the moment:

  • Westside Road to Klapatche Park (overnighter)
  • Northern Loop (4-day loop)
  • Gobblers Knob
  • Eagle Peak
  • Shriner Peak
  • Palisades Lakes

At some point I want to complete the entire Wonderland. Perhaps 2021?

This site wil be updated as and when I hike these or other Rainier trails.
