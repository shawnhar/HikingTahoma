Future Plans

My goal is to hike every maintained trail inside Mount Rainier National Park. This goal is now, if not quite yet in sight, at least close enough that I can enumerate all the trails I have still to explore.

Backpacking trips:

  • The entire Wonderland! (I plan to try for a permit in 2021)
  • Eastside Loop
  • Huckleberry Creek
  • Westside Road to North Puyallup

Day hikes:

  • Paul Peak Trail
  • Crystal Peak
  • Grand Park from Lake Eleanor
  • Naches Peak Loop
  • Pinnacle Saddle
  • Upper Eastside Trail
  • Silver Forest Trail

Annoying little bits and pieces needed for completeness:

  • High Lakes Trail
  • Narada Falls Trail (from Narada Falls to the Lakes Loop)
  • Grindstone Trail (short trail section along Mowich Lake Road)

This site will be updated with increasingly more of the map colored in as I continue to work my way through this list.
