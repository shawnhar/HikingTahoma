Future Plans

My goal is to hike every maintained trail inside Mount Rainier National Park. This goal is now, if not quite yet in sight, at least close enough that I can enumerate all the trails I have still to explore.

Backpacking trips:

  • The entire Wonderland! (I have a permit for September 2021)
  • Eastside PCT Loop (via Laughingwater Creek and Chinook Pass)
  • Eastside Loop
  • Gobblers Knob to Goat Lake or Mount Beljica
  • Huckleberry Creek

Day hikes:

  • Cowlitz Divide Trail
  • Crystal Peak
  • Naches Peak Loop
  • Owyhigh Lakes from Deer Creek
  • Pinnacle Saddle
  • Silver Forest Trail

Things I want to do for fun, but that don't count toward my formal goal as they are not officially maintained trails:

  • Backbone Ridge
  • Deadwood Lakes
  • Knapsack Pass
  • Skyscraper Mountain
  • West Fork White River

This site will be updated with increasingly more of the map colored in as I continue to work my way through this list.
